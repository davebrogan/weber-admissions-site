Virtual Campus Tour Videos

Welcome to the virtual video campus tour of Weber State University. We’ve highlighted several locations on campus to give you a taste of why we think Weber is great, Great, GREAT!

We recommend that you begin your tour at the #1 pindrop and follow the numbers, but really, you can watch the rest however you want. Don’t miss out on the bonus videos throughout the experience that will give you more information on specific departments, programs, student resources and more!
